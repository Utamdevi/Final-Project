About Fit Nation

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by fitness enthusiasts Alex and Jamie, Fit Nation started as a small 1000 sq ft studio with just 5 machines and a dream to make fitness accessible to everyone.

Today, we've grown into a 20,000 sq ft fitness haven serving over 5000 members. Our philosophy is simple: Fitness should be accessible, enjoyable, and transformative. We believe in celebrating every milestone, no matter how small.
